About

I am a PhD candidate in Combinatorics & Optimization working on cryptography at the University of Waterloo, advised by Prof. David Jao. My research analyzes the security of post-quantum protocols built from isogenies and algebraic codes, with a focus on digital signatures. I also apply quantum algorithmic methods to classical problems in computational number theory.

I currently organize the Isogeny Research Meeting within Dr. David Jao's research lab, as part of the Act2Qrypt initiative.
